About Cadan restaurant

Cadan restaurant provides quick and friendly service to locals and visitors in Hamarweyne. We focus on simple, well-seasoned fast foods, comforting lung foods, and a small cafette for coffee and snacks. We aim for fresh ingredients, fair prices, and a clean welcoming space.
